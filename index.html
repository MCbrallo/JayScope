About JScope

Inspiring • Trustworthy • Scientific

JScope connects learners, creators, and researchers to a global telescope network. Our platform schedules, safeguards, and calibrates every capture so you can focus on discovery.

Global access • Fair pricing • Priority scheduling • Real image pipeline • You own your data

How it works

1. Choose a telescope

Filter by aperture, location, filters, and seeing conditions.

2. Book your window

Fair, weather-aware queue. Priority depends on plan.

3. Receive your data

Calibrated pipeline (darks/flats/bias). JPEG/PNG or RAW/FITS by tier.
