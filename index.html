About JayScope

Inspiring • Trustworthy • Scientific

JayScope connects learners, creators, and researchers to a global telescope network. Book observing windows in minutes and receive scientifically processed data ready to explore, publish, or print.

Global access • Fair pricing • Priority scheduling • Real image pipeline • You own your data

How it works

1. Choose a telescope

Browse by aperture, location, filters, and seeing conditions.

2. Book your window

Priority depends on plan. Our queue & scheduler optimize for fairness and weather.

3. Receive your data

Calibrated images (darks/flats/bias). Download JPEG/PNG or RAW/FITS by tier.
