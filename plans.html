FAQ

Our scheduler balances plan priority with fairness and weather windows. Missed sessions due to weather are rebooked automatically.
If weather prevents your session, we rebook at no cost. If rebooking is unsuitable, we credit your hours back.
You own your output data. JayScope provides processing but your captures remain yours. See Terms for attribution guidelines.
All partner telescopes implement telemetry & safe limits. Operators retain an immediate remote stop (kill-switch).
Pipelines include darks, flats, and bias frames. Observer and Researcher tiers support custom sets and calibration scheduling.
Processed images are available shortly after capture. RAW/FITS bundles are queued for delivery with progress in your dashboard.
